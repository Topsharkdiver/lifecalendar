What it does

LifeCalendar quietly turns real moments into meaningful calendar events, so you can see where your time really goes — without manual tracking.

  • Creates calendar events based on real places and moments
  • Helps you read your week by time, places, and boundaries
  • Scan documents and anchor them to specific moments (optional reminders)
  • Works with Apple Calendar + Location APIs
“Life happens in time. We only make it visible.”

Privacy-first by design

Your data stays on your device and in your iCloud account. If the app disappears, your life history remains — safely stored in your calendar.
